# Pricing Principles

Psilodigital pricing should reflect the value delivered to the client's operations, not the hours spent.

## How we price

- Fixed-price for well-scoped projects when possible.
- Time and materials only when scope is genuinely uncertain.
- Optional support/retainer after delivery.
- Monthly run cost separated from one-time build cost.

## What we avoid

- Race-to-the-bottom day rates.
- Padding hours.
- Hidden costs surfacing late.
- Quoting before we understand the operation.

## What every quote includes

- Outcome we are buying.
- What's in scope.
- What's explicitly NOT in scope.
- Assumptions.
- Estimated timeline.
- Payment milestones.
