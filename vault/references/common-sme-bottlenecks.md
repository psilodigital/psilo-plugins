# Common SME Bottlenecks

A quick reference of bottlenecks Psilodigital sees most often in SMBs.

## Information

- Data scattered across spreadsheets, email, WhatsApp, paper.
- No single source of truth for customers, jobs, or inventory.
- Critical information lives in one person's head.

## Workflow

- Manual steps between tools that should be connected.
- Repetitive data entry across systems.
- Long handoffs between team members.
- Tasks tracked in chat threads, not a tracker.

## Customer-facing

- Inconsistent communication with customers.
- Manual confirmations and reminders.
- Slow response times on inquiries.
- No structured post-sale follow-up.

## Visibility

- Owner can't answer "how was last month?" with a number.
- Reports are built from scratch each time.
- No early warning for problems.

## Tools

- Too many subscriptions, partially used.
- Tools that nobody on the team actually opens.
- Old systems nobody dares to replace.
- One contractor or person holds all the keys.

## People

- Founder doing operational work that should be delegated.
- Onboarding new staff takes weeks because nothing is written down.
- Decisions stuck because the owner is the bottleneck.
