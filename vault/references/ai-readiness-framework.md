# AI Readiness Framework

A simple framework Psilodigital uses to assess whether a business is ready to benefit from AI assistance.

## Level 0 — Not ready

- Information is scattered.
- Processes are tribal.
- No clear workflows to assist.
- AI would be applied to chaos and produce more chaos.

Recommended: structure operations first, then revisit AI.

## Level 1 — Foundations

- Core data is centralized (CRM, booking system, internal admin).
- Key workflows are documented.
- Team has shared tools they actually use.

AI can start helping in narrow, low-risk areas: drafting replies, summarizing notes, classifying inbound.

## Level 2 — Assisted operations

- Workflows are stable.
- Knowledge is curated.
- Roles are clear.

AI agents can take over routine drafting, routing, follow-ups, and analysis with human approval.

## Level 3 — AI-native operations

- Operations explicitly designed around human + AI collaboration.
- Clear governance for what AI can do autonomously.
- Measurable outcomes from AI involvement.

This is the long-term target. Most SMBs land at Level 1 or 2.

## How we use this

- Place the client at a level.
- Honest assessment, not flattery.
- The first project usually moves them up one level — not three.
