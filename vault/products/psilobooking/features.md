# PsiloBooking Features

## Availability

- Real-time availability calendar
- Per-product and per-resource capacity
- Seasonal schedules and blackout dates

## Bookings

- Online booking via embedded widget
- Manual bookings from the back office
- Customer self-service confirmation page

## Customer management

- Customer profiles
- Booking history
- Notes and tags

## Communication

- Automated booking confirmations
- Pre-trip reminders
- Post-trip follow-ups

## Operations

- Source tracking (direct, OTA, walk-up)
- Daily manifest view
- Refund and reschedule handling

## Reporting

- Bookings per period, per source, per product
- Revenue overview
- Occupancy view
