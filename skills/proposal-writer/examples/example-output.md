# Example Output — Proposal Writer

Client: Artway
Project: Artist Profile & Press Kit System

## 1. Executive summary

Artway needs a faster, less manual way to keep artist profiles and press kits up to date. We propose a focused first release: a structured artist profile system with a simple internal workflow, integrated with the existing website. The goal is to remove the back-and-forth around press kits and give the team a single place to manage artist information.

## 2. Current situation

Artist information is collected by email and shared via Google Drive links. Updates to the public website depend on manual coordination between the team and a developer. Booking inquiries are scattered.

## 3. Business challenges

- Manual collection of press kit materials.
- Slow updates to public artist information.
- No single source of truth for artist data.
- Booking inquiries hard to track and follow up on.

## 4. Proposed solution

A lightweight internal admin where the team manages artists, bios, photos, audio, and press kits. The same data powers the public website. Inquiries are captured into a shared view.

## 5. Scope

### Core scope
- Internal artist admin (CRUD)
- Press kit fields and asset uploads
- Approval flow before public publish
- Public artist profile pages on the site
- Booking inquiry form with shared inbox

### Out of scope
- Full CRM replacement
- Multi-language artist profiles (phase 2)
- Payment processing
- Mobile app

## 6. Optional additions

- AI-assisted bio drafting (low effort, medium value)
- Press kit PDF export (low effort, high value)
- Analytics dashboard (medium effort, medium value)

## 7. Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery & Design | 1–2 weeks | Final scope, designs, data model |
| Build | 5–7 weeks | Working system in staging |
| Launch & Handover | 1 week | Live on production, team trained |

## 8. Assumptions

- The website CMS allows the integration we need. To be confirmed in discovery.
- The team is willing to use a new internal admin daily.
- Media assets are below the size limits of standard cloud storage.
